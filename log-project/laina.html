Log Reflection

by Laina S

Posted on 4.7.16

Log’s aren’t as hard as they sound.

They were kind of confusing at first, but once Mr. B explained it in detail, I caught on pretty fast.

Just like when we were learning how to understand FOIL, I created a little acronym for changing log equations into exponential equations, and vise versa.

My acronym goes, “big B to the little e equals big R.” I know, it probably seems like it doesn’t make much sense, but I say it in my head every single time i’m dealing with log equations.

Mathematics is not about numbers, equations, computations, or algorithms, but simply just understanding.” -William Paul Thurston
