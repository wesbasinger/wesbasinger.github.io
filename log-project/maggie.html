My Opinion about Logs in Algebra 2

by Maggie Hinojosa

Posted on April 7, 2016

Logarithms are easy, you just have to pay attention!!!

The exponents are the easiest parts for me because all you do is multiply the base by itself however many times the exponent is.

Something that confuses me about Logarithms is when you’re giving a variable in a specific place and you have to work backwards.

REMEMBER!!! If you are given a problem with a negative exponet, then you’re going to be left with a fraction

Math. A place where someone can buy 82 watermelons and no one questions why…

When learning about Logarithms I suggest using different color pens to represent each number so don’t get confused.
