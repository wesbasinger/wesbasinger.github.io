My experience using logs and exponents

by Julien Gorena

Posted on April 7, 2016

My experience with exponents and logs have been extremely good. I have picked up on them both fairly easy.

Exponents have been especially easy because I have worked on these in the past. Basically, I’ve had a lot of practice with these and have had no problems thus far.

Logs were a bit more difficult simply because I’ve had no prior experience, but they still came fairly easy for me. Once you understand the basic patterns of flipping the numbers around, it becomes a much easier concept to grasp.

Overall, I’m having a good experience with exponents and logs and I’m hoping I can continue to master these concepts in the future.
