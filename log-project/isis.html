The basics

by Isis Toldson

Posted on 04/07/16

Learning about logs was to begin with very hard. I didn’t understand the concept of moving things in equations around for no reason. I soon learned that there was a reason, but that didn’t make it any easier to fully comprehend.

After the first week I understood the concept of putting thing in order so I could solve the equation. It became less stressful, and definitely much easier to understand.
